Highlights

what to do in Galway

The Spanish Arch

This surviving fragment of the city walls once served as a checkpoint for nautical travellers. Look out for the magnificent Galway Hookers, traditional Irish boats, sailing around the harbour out into the blue of Galway Bay. Today quirky shops, serenading buskers and a diverse selection of restaurants bring the cobbled streets to life.

Galway City Museum

With it's over 1,000 historic objects on display, which has plenty of information to feed your curious mind. Spend a leisurely afternoon browsing the exhibits.

Waterways walk

If you wish to take a pause from the bustling Street of Galway, we recommend go to Waterways Walk. continue to the West End with its lively pubs and top music venues. An ideal spot for a panoramic view of the sunset if you time it right.
