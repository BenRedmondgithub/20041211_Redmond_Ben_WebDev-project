Coastal Comfort B&B

Stay a while

Every room feels like home. Coastal Comfort B&B has been furnished with the aim of making our guests feel like family. with a private bathroom that is furnished with a walnut wood floor, a heating system, an iron, A room ironing board, a hairdryer, a flat-screen TV from 32” to 40”, and tea & coffee-making facilities. Breakfast is included, and other facilities are; high-speed wireless internet connection available in all the properties, and private off-street parking, and all taxes included.

Cozy place in the heart of Galway

Coastal Comfort B&B is located in a convenient location whereby one can easily access all that they want to do in Galway. Some of the places that are easily accessible from the property include the Eyre Square Shopping Centre, two sandy beaches, convenient access to coastal walks, several restaurants, and pubs where one can enjoy traditional Irish music.
