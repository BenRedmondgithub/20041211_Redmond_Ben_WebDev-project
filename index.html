Coastal Comfort B&B

Stay a while

Every room is our B&B is designed to provide guests with ultimate comfort and convenience. Each room features elegant furnishings, is entirely non-smoking, and includes a private bathroom equipped with a full-sized bathtub and a separate walk-in tropical rain shower. Guests can enjoy the warmth of walnut wood flooring, central heating, and additional amenities such as an iron, ironing board, and hairdryer. Entertainment is provided with flat-screen TVs ranging from 32" to 40", along with tea and coffee-making facilities. The rate also covers a hearty breakfast, complimentary 24-hour high-speed wireless Internet access throughout the property, free private off-street parking, and all applicable taxes.

Cozy place in the heart of Galway

Conveniently located, the B&B offers easy access to a range of nearby attractions and activities. Guests can explore the Eyre Square Shopping Centre, relax on two nearby sandy beaches, or enjoy stunning coastal walks. A variety of restaurants are within walking distance, alongside welcoming pubs featuring traditional Irish music. Outdoor enthusiasts can take advantage of horse-riding, golfing, fishing, and an array of water sports, with bike rentals available for added exploration. Discounted sightseeing tours to popular destinations such as Connemara, the Cliffs of Moher, the Aran Islands, and a bus tour of Galway City can be arranged directly at the B&B, with tickets available upon arrival. Located 40 miles (80 km) from Shannon Airport and roughly a two-hour drive from Dublin Airport, the B&B also provides taxi services upon request. The nearest bus stop is conveniently situated just 50 yards away.
