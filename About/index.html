About Us

Meet Gillian

Gillian Moore is the proud owner of a charming and unique Bed & Breakfast nestled on the picturesque west coast of Ireland in Galway City. With a passion for hospitality and a love for her local heritage, Gillian has created a warm and welcoming retreat where guests can experience the best of Irish culture and comfort. Her B&B combines luxurious accommodations with a personal touch, offering visitors an unforgettable stay amidst Galway’s vibrant atmosphere, stunning coastal views, and rich traditions. Whether it’s sharing insider tips about the area or ensuring every detail is perfect, Gillian’s dedication shines through in every aspect of her business.
